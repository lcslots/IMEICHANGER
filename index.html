ANNOUNCEMENT TIPS!

Why Some Promos May Not Work:

Some internet promos (like NSD) may not work on PLDT modems, even with signal, due to restrictions set by the ISP (Smart). These promos are intended for personal use only and cannot be used on 5G modems.

Recommended Solution: Use promos such as UNLIFAM1299 (SMARTBRO PLDT HOME SIM), which are compatible with PLDT modems. If you have a Smart/Sun/TNT SIM with UNLIDATA (like UNLIDATA649), this will work as it is not restricted for personal use.
